Financial Support
for Families

The Canada Child Benefit provides monthly payments to help families with the cost of raising children under 18. This tax-free benefit is income-tested and varies based on the number of children and family income, offering crucial support to ensure children have a good start in life.

Guaranteed Income for Seniors

The Old Age Security program offers a monthly pension to Canadians aged 65 and older, providing a stable income in retirement. Eligibility is based on residency in Canada, and the amount received may be supplemented by additional benefits for low-income seniors to ensure financial security in their later years.
